FAQ

Frequently asked questions about the photo studio planner app.

Here are the most useful answers before preparing a stage plan, loading a preset, checking distances or exporting a lighting diagram.

What is Lumiere Studio for?

The app lets you build a photo or video studio plan with a top view, an isometric preview, an element library, presets and export of the main plan.

Can I use it on mobile?

Yes. The app has been reworked to stay usable on phones, with a responsive layout, pinch zoom and compact controls.

What measurements are available?

Measure mode can automatically show the distances between the subject, the camera, the backdrop and the other elements. You can also draw a manual measurement.

Can I zoom the top view?

Yes. Zoom works with the buttons, the mouse wheel on desktop and pinch on mobile without breaking placement or measurements.

Can I save my own presets?

Yes. In addition to the built in presets, you can save your custom variants in the browser from the Presets menu.

Can I export PDF?

Yes. The File menu lets you copy the image, export PNG, export PDF and print the top view.

Can I change the language?

Yes. The public site and the app support French, English, Spanish, German and Arabic.
