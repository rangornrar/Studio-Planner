Presets

Studio presets for quick starts and custom variants that preserve your workflow.

Presets give you a credible starting point. You can then adjust distances, furniture, colors or power and save your own version in the browser.

Portrait, beauty and corporate

Efficient bases for headshots, editorial portraits, beauty and professional portrait work.

Examples: soft 45 degree portrait, clamshell beauty, clean corporate.

Fashion, product, group and interview

Structured presets for silhouettes, catalog work, products, duos, families or studio video.

Examples: parabolic fashion, tabletop packshot, LED interview.

Boudoir, fine art nude and creative

More graphic or sensitive scenes with furniture, stronger backdrops, projection and shadow play.

Examples: boudoir parabolic, hard sculptural nude, shadow play.
